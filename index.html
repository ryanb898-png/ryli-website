Questions, Answered

Before you go live.

Does RYLI replace OBS?
No. RYLI is a desktop companion that works alongside OBS. It gives you live show data, a voice co-host, and a browser-source overlay for your existing stream setup — you keep using OBS exactly as you do today.
Do my viewers need to install anything?
No. Your audience just sees the overlay in your broadcast, same as any other on-screen graphic. RYLI runs entirely on your own computer.
Is my show data private?
Yes. Everything runs locally on your PC. RYLI automatically detects and only logs your own stream — if you raid out or watch someone else's show, logging pauses until your show is back, so your data never mixes with anyone else's.
Is RYLI Whatnot-only?
Today, yes — RYLI is purpose-built for Whatnot livestream selling. More platforms may come later.
Does RYLI run on Mac?
Not yet — RYLI is a Windows desktop app today.
What happens after my free trial?
Your core overlay — themes, leaderboards, milestone celebrations, the announcer, and your all-time/live Insights stats — stays free forever. Pro-only features (talking to Ryli, your Top buyers & Suggested outreach boards, premium themes, Ultra FX, webcam, premium voices, custom banners) require a subscription to keep using after the 30-day trial.
