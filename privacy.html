Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

This policy covers the RYLI desktop application and the ryli.app website (together, "RYLI," "we," or "us"). It explains what information we collect, where it lives, and who else ever sees it. If you have questions, reach us anytime at hello@ryli.app.

The short version

RYLI is built to be local-first. Your show data — chat, bids, buyer history, sales — is read and stored entirely on your own computer. We don't run a server that collects it, store it in any database of ours, or sell it to anyone. The only information that leaves your computer is what's strictly needed to run your license/subscription and to check for app updates, described below.

What the RYLI app collects

Your show data stays on your PC. RYLI reads publicly visible information from the Whatnot browser tab you're already using to host your show — chat messages, bids, buyer usernames, sale amounts, viewer counts — and saves it to a file on your own computer so the app can show you leaderboards, celebrations, and Insights. This data is never uploaded to us. It's yours; if you delete it from your computer, it's gone.

License and trial status. To run your 30-day free trial and validate a Pro subscription, RYLI communicates with our payment processor, Lemon Squeezy, sending only what's needed to check whether a license key is valid. See Lemon Squeezy's own privacy policy for how they handle that.

App updates and the Stream Store. RYLI checks GitHub to see if a newer version is available, and to download optional Stream Store content (overlay effects, Stream Deck icon packs) if you choose to use them. These are standard file-download requests and don't include your show data or personal information.

Optional research feature (Pro). If you choose to connect your own Claude or OpenAI API key so Ryli can look up product or market information live on air, your questions are sent directly from your computer to that provider using your own key — not through any RYLI server. This is entirely optional and off by default.

What we don't do. RYLI does not run background analytics or telemetry, does not track your usage of the app, and does not access your microphone or webcam data beyond what's needed to run the features you've turned on, locally, in the moment. Nothing is sent to advertisers.

What the ryli.app website collects

The website itself does not currently use any tracking or analytics scripts. If you submit an email address through a signup form on this site, that address is used only to contact you about RYLI (for example, a launch announcement) and is processed through our form provider, Formspree.

Third parties we work with

  • Lemon Squeezy — handles subscription billing and license validation.
  • GitHub — hosts app updates and optional Stream Store content.
  • Anthropic / OpenAI — only if you personally connect your own API key for the optional research feature; your requests go directly to them using your own credentials.
  • Formspree — only if you submit an email through a form on this website.

We don't sell, rent, or share your information with anyone else, and we don't use any of these services to build advertising profiles.

Data security

Because your show data lives on your own computer rather than a server we control, its security depends significantly on your own computer's security — the same as any locally-installed application. We recommend normal precautions (keeping your OS and antivirus up to date, not sharing your license key).

Children's privacy

RYLI is not directed at children and isn't intended for use by anyone under 18. We don't knowingly collect information from children.

Your choices

Since your show history lives on your own computer, you're always in control of it — you can view, back up, or delete it directly from your files at any time. To stop a Pro subscription or remove a license key, use the License tab inside the app, or contact Lemon Squeezy directly for billing-related requests.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continuing to use RYLI after a change means you accept the updated policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy or your data? Email hello@ryli.app.
